About Me

As a UI/UX Designer with a background in healthcare, I blend technical expertise with a passion for improving user experiences. My career began as a Respiratory Therapist at renowned hospitals such as Taipei City Hospital and Changhua Christian Hospital, where I was involved in medical system development, user research, and patient education. I led projects like the "Cross Island Collaborative Home Care Project," which earned third place in the 2018 City Government Creative Proposal Competition. These experiences have honed my ability to merge user needs with technical solutions, and I specialize in creating intuitive, accessible designs that meet the unique challenges of healthcare environments.

In addition to my healthcare experience, I am a Freelance Medical Editor, where I support medical professionals by editing and refining content for accuracy and clarity. During my CareerFoundry training, I expanded my skill set to include wireframing, prototyping, accessibility design, and responsive web design. I’m passionate about staying on the cutting edge of technology, continuously exploring new developments in front-end development and UI/UX design. When not designing, I enjoy outdoor activities like skiing, mountain biking, and kayaking, and I’m also an aspiring chef, always experimenting with new recipes and technology. My diverse background allows me to approach design challenges from a multidisciplinary perspective, ensuring solutions that enhance both functionality and user satisfaction.
